Jhonny Silverhand

Before Samurai disbanded in 2008, lead singer Johnny Silverhand was a well-known rockerboy. He was a former soldier who battled the corrupted NUSA government and megacorporations, but he was also a recognised manipulator, irrational, and impulsive person. He was murdered by Adam Smasher in 2023's Night City Holocaust, but his conscience was encoded into the Relic before his physical functions stopped.

Judy Alvarez

The Mox's Judy Alvarez is a proficient braindance technician. Watson, she lives independently in northeastern Kabuki. V might potentially find love with Judy in Cyberpunk 2077.

x

Jackie Welles

A Heywood-born mercenary named Jackie Welles linked up with V to become Night City legends. He serves as one of V's first lines of contact and the player's comrade on a number of missions. Above all else, he treasured family, friends, and loyalty. He planned to support his own family and enjoy a luxurious lifestyle eventually, but he grew frustrated because this was still only a distant fantasy.

x

Misty Olwezewski

A new-age spiritualist, Misty Olszewski is the proprietor of Misty's Esoterica, a shop on Watson's Urmland Street. She is Jackie Welles' girlfriend and a close friend of Viktor Vektor and V.

x

Evelyn Parker

Evelyn is a mysterious beauty. Evelyn appears to be a high-ranking corpo, yet she sets up a meeting in a dive pub frequented by street gangs. At first impression, she is nothing but a maze of paradoxes and inconsistencies. She looks to be a wealthy, sophisticated woman, yet she gathers a motley crew of inexperienced mercenaries to steal something for her. She's funny and endearing, but she demands a lot. puts her trust in experts yet still wants to be in control.Only one thing is clear when it comes to Evelyn: be cautious. Her charm, wit, and tenacity combine to create a dangerous combination.

x

Meredith Stout

Senior operations manager for Militech was Meredith Stout. In 2077, a Militech convoy was hijacked by the Maelstrom gang as a result of a company leak. The corporation, suspecting inside information, tasked Meredith with locating the leak. Soon after, she discovered evidence that pointed to her main suspect, Anthony Gilchrist, who was swiftly apprehended and questioned by her team. Meredith, however, was not provided with sufficient information or proof. She started desperately searching Watson for any leads as her job and life were on the line.
